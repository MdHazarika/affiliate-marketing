About Us

Welcome to AFFILUX CF


AFFILUX CF is an affiliate product discovery platform created to help people find the best products at the best prices in the least amount of time. Our goal is simple — make online shopping easier, faster, and smarter.


Founded by Aman Das and Md. A. R. Hazarika, AFFILUX CF was built by developers who wanted to simplify the way people discover products online. Instead of spending hours searching across multiple platforms, our website helps users quickly find quality products and directly access them through trusted online stores.


Our Mission


Our mission is to help people make better buying decisions by showcasing useful products, trending gadgets, and fashion items from reliable online marketplaces.


We focus on:


  • Finding useful and trending products
  • Helping users discover the best value for money
  • Saving users time when searching for products online

What We Do


AFFILUX CF collects and presents products from popular e-commerce platforms such as Amazon and other trusted shopping apps.


When you browse our website, you can:


  • Discover fashion products and gadgets
  • Explore useful and trending items
  • Click the provided links to purchase directly from trusted marketplaces

We aim to expand into more product categories in the future as our platform grows.


Why Trust Us


At AFFILUX CF, we focus on simplicity, transparency, and usefulness.


  • ✔ Curated product listings
  • ✔ Easy product discovery
  • ✔ Fast access to trusted marketplaces
  • ✔ Clean and simple browsing experience

Our goal is not just to list products, but to help users find what they need quickly and efficiently.


Affiliate Disclosure


AFFILUX CF participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click on certain links on our website and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.


These commissions help us maintain and improve the platform while continuing to provide useful product recommendations.
